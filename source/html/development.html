DOUBLE WIN

 

We’ve had another great year to celebrate at Haygarth and turning 30 years old has been a momentous milestone. However, we’re not ones to rest on our laurels, especially when it comes to creativity, which is why we are proud to be celebrating a double win at both ‘The Grocer’ and ‘FMBE’ awards. Our fantastic Gillette Movember work picked up most Effective Integration/Amplification campaign at the FMBE’s and we also won The Grocer Brand Experience Agency of the year – our 4th consecutive year of winning no less! Being a proud bunch, we’ve wrapped up these achievements and work that excites us on a brand new website and a shiny new showreel – why not take a look.
